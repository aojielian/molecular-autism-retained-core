Supplementary Figure S5 legend
Supplementary Figure S5. Workflow overview of the retained-core discovery, cross-cohort validation, and external bulk-cortex analysis framework.
The schematic summarizes the full analytic workflow used in this study. The left panel shows discovery-stage processing in the PsychENCODE/Wamsley cortical resource, including global cortical cell-type context, microglial extraction, all-cluster audit, retained-core restriction to clusters 0 and 2, donor-level abundance/composition analyses, donor-paired internal-state testing, and pseudobulk differential-expression analyses. The middle panel shows cross-cohort validation in the Velmeshev single-nucleus dataset, including validation microglia extraction, reclustering, weak mapped-cluster comparison to discovery clusters 0 and 2, frozen candidate/reference program scoring, seven prespecified score-based metrics, and directional-concordance robustness analyses. The right panel shows independent external validation in the Gandal 2022 bulk-cortex dataset, including projection of frozen candidate and reference programs, candidate-minus-reference scoring, mixed-model diagnosis testing, and leave-one-region-out sensitivity analysis. Supplementary perturbation-based and TF-prioritization analyses are shown as downstream mechanistic-context layers rather than as primary evidence.
